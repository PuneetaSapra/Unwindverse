ABOUT US

Often people get confused about their thoughts and feelings. Due
to this, they fail to decide what should they do about it.
In this project, we will help people by giving clarity about their mood.
After this, we will suggest them some interactive activities according to their
current mood by using machine learning approaches
that will help them to get a better understanding of
their mental health which will further help them to do correct activities accordingly.
